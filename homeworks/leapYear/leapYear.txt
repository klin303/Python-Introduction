Leap Year Homework Spec:
- The purpose of this program is to determine if a given year is a leap year.
- A leap year is defined as:
    - A leap year is exactly divisible by 4 except for century years (years ending in 00)
    - A year is also a leap year if it is divisible by 400


Sample Inputs and Outputs:


>>> 2001
<<< 2001 is not a leap year.

>>> 1900
<<< 1900 is not a leap year.

>>> 2012
<<< 2012 is a leap year.

>>> 2000
<<< 2000 is a leap year.
